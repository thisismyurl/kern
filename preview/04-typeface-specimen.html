Use it to explain your own typeface choices, or as a reference for how Kern's typographic decisions were made.

Plus Jakarta Sans — Body & UI Voice

Plus Jakarta Sans at base size, weight 400, line-height 1.7. This is the reading weight — the one used for all body copy, post content, and sidebar text. The 1.7 line-height is a deliberate choice: studio portfolios are read carefully, not scanned. Give the type room to breathe.
